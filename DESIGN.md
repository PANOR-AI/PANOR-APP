# PANOR Medical Design System

This design system is engineered for the **PANOR Autonomous Enterprise Healthcare System**, designed to communicate clinical authority, absolute data trust, and state-of-the-art AI intelligence.

## Palette
- **Primary Indigo (#312E81):** Dominant corporate clinical navy. Used for structural blocks, headers, and major typography.
- **AI Purple (#7C3AED):** Represents Google Antigravity and Vertex AI agent intelligence. Used for AI highlights, reasoning traces, and copilot panels.
- **Emergency Red (#DC2626):** High-visibility red for critical vitals warnings, orange/red triage overlays, and cardiac hard-stops.
- **Success Emerald (#059669):** High-trust green for normal vitals, validated SOAP drafts, and successful lab test releases.
- **Clinical White (#FFFFFF):** Main card base, creating spacious, clean clinical panels.
- **Soft Slate (#F8FAFC):** Application background, delivering a soothing, premium canvas.

## Typography
- **Headings (Outfit):** Modern, geometric, clean. Sets a premium, high-tech, medical-grade tone.
- **Body & Controls (Inter):** Highly legible, high-density reading. Used for clinical charts, differential details, and lab lists.
- **Data & Logs (JetBrains Mono):** Standard for telemetry feeds, token tracking, audit logs, and agent confidence levels.

## Elevation & Shapes
- **Corner Rounding:** 12px for modern cards and dialogs; 8px for buttons and form fields.
- **Borders:** Subtle 1px lines (#E2E8F0) to organize data cleanly without heavy visual clutter.
- **Shadows:** Soft, diffused elevation profiles mimicking high-end iOS/Apple Health surfaces.
